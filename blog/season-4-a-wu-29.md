# Champions Cup

And with The League (tm) coming to its end... welcome to the Champions Cup! Our final tournament of the season in which it has been decided (by the voters on Discord) to do a combined league tournament to close it out!

### The Group Stage

To start will be a 5 week group stage in which 4 groups of 6 will battle it out to qualify for the knockout rounds! The top 4 from each group will move on, and the bottom 2 will be eliminated! These groups have been crafted with 3 managers from each league, pulled from 3 different pots (Pot 1: #1-#4, Pot 2: #5-#8, Pot 3: #9-#12). And have resulted in the following groups:

### The Knockout Rounds

The knockout rounds will consist of another 5 weeks: a single elimination bracket starting in the Round of 16 and culminating in a two-legged final in gameweeks 37 and 38! And, of course, how well you do in your group determines where in the bracket you end up, so every group stage match matters!

Oh, and in the case of knockout round ties, this is how we will handle it:

- Number of goals scored by team - Number of saves made by opposition goalkeeper

For example, if Team A has 3 goals and Team B's goalkeeper has 2 saves, Team A will have a score of 1. You get the gist. And in further case of tie I'll have to brainstorm some more whacky ideas to try, which will probably look something like:

- Total number of assists
- Total sum of BPS
- Most minutes played
- Highest scoring player in the matchup

Anyway, just some ideas and feel free to let me know if y'all come up with anything else I should consider!
