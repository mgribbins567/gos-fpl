Not a ton of movement this week (unfortunately), but let's recap! John's win means he secures the League B title with just a 1 point lead over me! Alex and Chris end their solid seasons in 3rd and 4th, with a two point gap between them. Morgs and ZachA's run of form to end the season saw them jump over Mack and take home 5th and 6th place, dropping her down to 7th. Emma finishes her campaign with a respectable 8th place finish, with Cara not too terribly far behind. Andrew's up and down (and also rather unlucky) season saw a 10th place finish, 4 points ahead of Travis, who is followed by Divya!

Game Changers! The 10-screen set up proved itself worthy this week so I should have seen some action from every player here.

- Matthew: Harry Maguire - 6 pts - Slabhead follows up the disappointment from not being called up for the England squad (seriously, wtf?) with a clean sheet performance for United against Brighton. Solid.
- Morgs: Caoimhin Kelleher - 6 pts - Rare you see 2 bonus points for a keeper in a 1-1 match but the Irishman ended his season on a high with 7 saves as Brentford kept the door shut on Liverpool (mostly) this weekend.
- ZachA: Bryan Mbeumo - 9 pts - An 11 goal season for the bald ex-Brentford man as he netted one more for the road against Brighton.
- Chris: Ollie Watkins - 13 pts - What more to say than OLLIE OLLIE OLLIE OI OI OI as he bagged back to back braces to close out the season. When Aston Villa didn't have anything to play for. The best to ever do it.
- John: Bruno Fernandes - 14 pts - Mr. Manchester United himself sets the new Premier League assist record with a bang as he swung in a corner that landed right on Dorgu's forehead and was suspiciously quickly deemed _not_ a Bart own goal. I'm just joking he totally deserved the record after being forced to play as a DM for Amorim for half the season.
- Alex: Jarrod Bowen - 12 pts - As West Ham sadly got relegated, Bowen did his part, notching 9 goals and 13 assists this season and cementing his spot as one of the most consistent FPL performers of recent years. _But where will he go next?_

Congratulations to John for winning May Manager of the Month on PF! He defeated Chris by just 1 point, Alex by 2, and me, ZachA, and Morgs by way more than that many! This is his 3rd Manager of the Month award this season!
