Fell off: Chris

10 - Alex (NEW) - League B - Last 5: 37 (W), 36 (L), 39 (L), 43 (W), 60 (W) - A big week for Alex sees him back on this list for the first time since Gameweek 23. His heavily Bournemouth and Liverpool biased team seems to be working though, as he steadily has worked his way up the League B table. Interestingly, Truffert (118), Thiaw (114), and Szoboszlai (120) have very similar total point values this season. And this is despite the fact that Thiaw is a complete wildcard and sometimes decides that -1 point is the way to go, and Szoboszlai has been Liverpool's best player.

9 - Jesse (-) - League A - Last 5: 58 (W), 36 (W), 39 (W), 55 (W), 39 (W) - I'm not saying Jesse is lucky, but I do think it's perhaps a little bit _suspect_ that he has had wins in the last 5 matches at 36, 39, and 39 points. Maybe he has a finger on the scale a little bit :eyes: anyway, a wins a win and Jesse has been doing just enough to get them off the back of a Truffert 11 pointer, Casemiro goal, and Ampadu and Garner defcons. Unfortunately for him though, Chalobah's ankle decided to take a vacation in the Champions League this week, Liverpool drew against Tottenham (lol), and I'm really not sure what Pep has been going for recently re Cherki. Even still, Jesse has the power of the luck table, old man Casemiro, and the goat Jorgen Strand-Larsen on his side.

8 - Matthew (-1) - League A - Last 5: 70 (W), 38 (L), 27 (W), 42 (L), 38 (W) - A win... but at what cost... My team has been looking like mid-season Luke after the 70 pointer with the occasional Semenyo/Solanke goal propping me up. At least James Hill can keep a clean sheet and Mac Allister can win a free kick or score every once in a while. But still, I've been surviving off of Gakpo/Rayan/Matheus Fernandes 3 pointers the last few.

7 - Dylan (+1) - League A - Last 5: 41 (W), 32 (W), 48 (L), 40 (L), 62 (W) - Look at that, removing a 52 (L) and adding a 62 (W) - has Dylan turned a corner? A huge week going into the blank for Dylan off the back of 4 clean sheets out of his 4 playing defenders (one was benched, though), 2 goals and an assist from his midfielders, and a Sels 6 pointer sitting behind his Sa 0. Plus, Salah looked to be a nice mix of his old self and usual 2025/2026 self vs Galatasaray this week, before he got subbed for an injury.

6 - Zach (-1) - League A - Last 5: 54 (W), 52 (W), 54 (W), 31 (L), 33 (L) - Back to back weeks in the 30s for Zach as despite Sesko putting in work off the bench, his MID and FWD group has been floundering a bit. Saka and Rogers have been fairly invisible as of late, Damsgaard looked to be a one-hit wonder, and Bournemouth can't remember what it feels like to score a goal. At least he's only got _5_ players from last week blanking this one. Surely that won't lead to any tough decisions.

5 - Kevin (+1) - League A - Last 5: 45 (L), 63 (W), 37 (D), 51 (W), 45 (W) - Another very solid week from Kevin, despite suffering from a DCL penalty miss and a MID/FWD group that eerily resembles the scores that Zach's put up (just replace Sesko with Gyokeres). Unlike Zach, however, Kevin's GKP/DEF group picked up 28 points, with Bart nabbing a clean sheet, Thiaw picking up a clean sheet and defcon, Mitchell getting his own clean sheet, and Frimpong getting a timely substitution to hang onto his 6 points before Liverpool conceded to Spurs. It is looking a bit rough in the midfield department though, as defcon beasts Caicedo and Gravenberch both just have 1 defcon return in the last 5 weeks. Not a huge deal when JP is putting up 19 every few weeks.

4 - Mack (-) - League B - Last 5: 52 (W), 46 (W), 35 (W), 49 (W), 42 (W) - A full slate of W's for Mack as she slowly but surely climbs her way toward the promotion spots in League B. This week's 40+ pointer comes from the Nottingham Forest + Fulham clean sheets as the best DEF for defcon in the game Andersen hits the mark yet again, old man Casemiro continued his scoring ways, Gyokeres got on the score sheet from about 1 yard out, and Garner snuck in his own 12 defcon performance.

3 - John (-) - League B - Last 5: 46 (W), 64 (W), 41 (W), 59 (W), 36 (W) - A heavily Bruno Fernandes weighted team this week from John, as over half of his points came from the Portuguese player's two assists + Gabriel clean sheet. Other than Stach hitting defcon, not a lot else was going on. I guess it all works out when you have the 2nd highest scorer in the game.

2 - Darryan (-) - League A - Last 5: 24 (L), 46 (W), 43 (W), 65 (W), 34 (L) - A rough one this week for Darryan as Timber got subbed in the first half due to injury, Alex Jimenez didn't get his start, and 6 points from his FWD trio did not help the situation. Plus, he's staring down the barrel of a Saliba/Timber/Rodri/Haaland/Marmoush/Mosquera blank, so we might be seeing something of a roster swap this week so he can field a full squad. Or maybe he'll just tank the hit to his power ranking spot.

1 - Matthew2 (-) - League B - Last 5: 39 (L), 43 (W), 31 (L), 67 (W), 51 (L) - Not much you can say when you score the 2nd highest in the league and go up against the highest score. Even still, it's another 50+ week off the back of the double Bournemouth clean sheet, Bart doing Bart things, a few defcons from Ampadu, Rodri, and Ballard, and the future 2025/2026 golden boot winner Igor Thiago scoring his _19th_ goal of the season.
