Hello everyone and welcome to Game of Stones fantasy - World Cup edition! I'm finally back from my travels so I have a little bit of time to take a break from the Season 4 recap and write one of these, so let's get started.

Heading into Gameweek 3, there are only a handful of teams that have qualified for the Round of 32 already:

- Mexico - Group A
- United States - Group D
- Germany - Group E
- France - Group I
- Norway - Group I
- Argentina - Group J
- Colombia - Group K

and a slightly smaller sized handful have been eliminated:

- Haiti - Group C
- Turkey - Group D
- Tunisia - Group F
- Jordan - Group J
- Panama - Group L

**Group A**

1. Mexico (Q) - 6 pts
2. South Korea - 3 pts
3. Czechia - 1 pt
4. South Africa - 1 pt

- Mexico vs Czechia
- South Korea vs South Africa

Basically, South Korea qualifies in 2nd with a win (no matter the result of the Mexico game since Mexico wins H2H). Czechia/South Africa needs to win to have the chance to qualify. Czechia would _probably_ qualify in 3rd on 4 points if South Korea wins, but if South Africa wins it'll go down to goal difference for who is in 2nd vs 3rd (and again, 4 points is _probably_ enough).

**Group B**

1. Canada - 4 pts
2. Switzerland - 4 pts
3. Bosnia and Herzegovina - 1 pt
4. Qatar - 1 pt

- Switzerland vs Canada
- Bosnia and Herzegovina vs Qatar

Pretty simple, winner of Switzerland vs Canada wins the group. If they tie, Canada wins on GD. A loss for Canada likely has them qualify regardless of the result of Bosnia and Herzegovina vs Qatar, considering they have a +9 difference to BIH and H2H over Qatar. Plus, they are at +6 and 4 points so even if they end up in 3rd they're probably fine.

**Group C**

1. Brazil - 4 pts
2. Morocco - 4 pts
3. Scotland - 3 pts
4. Haiti (E) - 0 pts

- Brazil vs Scotland
- Morocco vs Haiti

RIP Haiti o7. Basically this group comes down to if Brazil beats Scotland and keeps their goal difference over Morocco, they finish 1st. Morocco wins and Brazil loses and they finish 1st. If Scotland wins, they go either 1st or 2nd depending on Morocco's result. CMON MCFRATM

**Group D**

1. United States (Q) - 6 pts
2. Australia - 3 pts
3. Paraguay - 3 pts
4. Turkiye (E) - 0 pts

- United States vs Turkiye
- Australia vs Paraguay

It's only fitting that **Freeman** and Balo**gun** won this group for the US as they go into a completely pointless match vs Turkiye. Australia vs Paraguay is the interesting one, where the winner qualifies in 2nd and the loser has to fight in the 3rd place gauntlet. A draw is honestly probably best for both teams but is a win for Australia because GD.

**Group E**

1. Germany (Q) - 6 pts
2. Ivory Coast - 3 pts
3. Ecuador - 1 pt
4. Curacao - 1 pt

- Ecuador vs Germany
- Ivory Coast vs Curacao

Germany has locked up 1st place so their game doesn't matter to them, but Ecuador can get up to 4 points with a win. Which could put them in 2nd if Curacao win. Maybe the most interesting result though would be a Germany win and a Curacao win, which would have the tiny island nation finish the group in 2nd with probably -5 GD.

**Group F**

1. Netherlands - 4 pts
2. Japan - 4 pts
3. Sweden - 3 pts
4. Tunisia (E) - 0 pts

- Netherlands vs Tunisia
- Japan vs Sweden

Really interesting that Tunisia fired their manager after losing vs Sweden considering this is quite the group of death, but whatever floats their boat I guess. Anyway, Netherlands can go first if they win and outscore Japan (likely), while Japan needs at least a draw against Sweden to secure 2nd. If they lose, they're probably through, but Sweden will be looking for a win to secure their place in the knockouts.

**Group G**

1. Egypt - 4 pts
2. Iran - 2 pts
3. Belgium - 2 pts
4. New Zealand - 1 pt

- Egypt vs Iran
- Belgium vs New Zealand

Probably the most interesting group here, considering Iran and Belgium both have 2 draws. And 1st place will come down to the **Pride Match** between Egypt and Iran. If Egypt wins, it's theirs. If Iran wins, it'll come down to GD vs Belgium (if they win), otherwise they take the group. The only team that can't finish 1st is New Zealand, who would need an Egypt win to finish 2nd and would otherwise finish 4th on 4 points (if they beat Belgium). Interesting stuff for sure.

**Group H**

1. Spain - 4 pts
2. Uruguay - 2 pts
3. Cape Verde - 2 pts
4. Saudi Arabia - 1 pt

- Spain vs Uruguay
- Cape Verde vs Saudi Arabia

All eyes on... Cape Verde as they have a genuine shot at qualifying for the Round of 32 this week. Spain looked pretty unstoppable against Saudi Arabia and Uruguay looked, well, stoppable against Cape Verde, so a win for Spain and for Cape Verde would see the Cape Verdeans through to the next round. The situation here is basically the same as Group G so just switch out the names above and re-read if you want the full rundown.

**Group I**

1. France (Q) - 6 pts
2. Norway (Q) - 6 pts
3. Senegal - 0 pts
4. Iraq - 0 pts

- France vs Norway
- Senegal vs Iraq

I mean, poor Senegal and Iraq having to defend against Mbappe and Haaland these past couple of weeks. But also it has set up a France vs Norway face off for 1st place in the group, with France winning if they draw. Senegal and Iraq have a long road ahead if they want to qualify, considering their GDs are at -3 and -6 respectively.

**Group J**

1. Argentina (Q) - 6 pts
2. Austria - 3 pts
3. Algeria - 3 pts
4. Jordan (E) - 0 pts

- Argentina vs Jordan
- Austria vs Algeria

Another group where the #1 will have nothing but records to play for as Argentina secured their 1st place spot this week. Austria and Algeria will battle it out to see which of them can secure 2nd on points. The good news is the loser will already be on 3 points. The bad news is they'll have a negative GD.

**Group K**

1. Colombia (Q) - 6 pts
2. Portugal - 4 pts
3. DR Congo - 1 pt
4. Uzbekistan - 0 pts

- Colombia vs Portugal
- DR Congo vs Uzbekistan

While Colombia has qualified for the Round of 32, they're one of the few teams on 6 points to _not_ have confirmed their #1 spot, so their head to head against Portugal in gameweek 3 will decide the group. DR Congo and Uzbekistan also aren't quite out, with a win putting them on 4 or 3 points, respectively, and giving them a shot at a 3rd place qualifying spot. Uzbekistan is certainly in a little bit worse of a position going into this considering the -7 GD.

**Group L**

1. England - 4 pts
2. Ghana - 4 pts
3. Croatia - 3 pts
4. Panama (E) - 0 pts

- England vs Panama
- Ghana vs Croatia

England goes into this last match with their Round of 32 spot basically secured, although it is completely possible they drop to 3rd if Panama batters them and Croatia only beats Ghana by a little bit. That said, what a game Ghana vs Croatia should be, with the winner securing at least 2nd place in the group. Although, England vs Ghana was incredibly boring so who even knows.
